Messy Intern [sic]

I just submitted
A spreadsheet to
My boss, who
Sent it out
To the whole
Department. I hope

It didn’t seem hopeless
Because I submitted
A list filled
with errors to
These busy lobbyists, all out
wondering who

on Earth they
are hoping
will figure out
The job; if they gave
the job to
Me, it’s probably

Not a huge
Deal if I
Mess it up.
They’d better hope
They also gave
This job

To someone more qualified
Who actually is up on
The amendments filed
And who
Has a hope
Of getting this right.

When the boss walks to
the big Hill, outside
in the humidity, I hope
they don’t fire
the intern who
Submitted

The sloppy spreadsheet, filled with hope whole
To shortcuts out -
who submitted this?!?
